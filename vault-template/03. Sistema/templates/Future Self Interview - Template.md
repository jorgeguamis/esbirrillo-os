This is a conversation with the person you're becoming.

The premise: imagine your future self — 3, 5, or 10 years from now — is sitting across from you. They've lived what you're about to live. They know how it turns out.

What would they tell you?

Set aside 45-60 minutes. Write this in a quiet space where you can think clearly.

## Notes

This exercise feels strange. That's normal.

You're not predicting the future. You're clarifying what you want and listening to the part of you that already knows the answer.

Your future self is not a different person. It's you, with more information and perspective.

What would that version of you say?

Listen.
